About Us

We are a UK based student company with the aim of
reducing global plastic waste.

We are dedicated to raising awareness of the PLIGHT
of our planet by marketing high-quality reusable bamboo straws.

We see our straws as a reminder
to everyone who purchases one
to be kinder to our planet.
The straws are a conversation starter
that ignites important discussions about
our world and the way that we treat it.
